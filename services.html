Our Services

Professional Renovation & Interior Design Services

From the first consultation to the final coat of paint, our renovation and interior design company delivers end-to-end property transformation — on time, on budget, and to the highest standard.

Painting & Interior Finishing

A fresh coat of paint is one of the fastest ways to transform a space — and one of the highest-ROI improvements before a sale or rental. We handle everything from surface preparation and crack repair to premium colour application and decorative finishes. Whether you want a clean neutral palette for a rental listing or a bold accent wall for a private residence, our team matches the right materials and technique to every surface. We work on apartments, offices, stairwells, and commercial spaces, delivering smooth, durable results that hold up over time and photograph beautifully for listings.

  • Surface prep, filling & priming
  • Interior & exterior wall painting
  • Decorative & textured finishes
  • Colour consultation included

General Apartment & Office Renovations

Comprehensive renovation work that covers every trade under one roof. We manage full-scope apartment and office refurbishments — from knocking down non-structural walls and laying new flooring to upgrading kitchens, bathrooms, and electrical layouts. Property owners benefit from a single point of contact across all contractors, a clear project timeline, and transparent cost breakdowns from day one. Whether you're refreshing a tired apartment for the rental market or completely reimagining an office floor for a new tenant, we plan, coordinate, and execute the entire project with minimal disruption.

  • Full-scope project management
  • Flooring, tiling & carpentry
  • Kitchen & bathroom upgrades
  • Electrical & plumbing coordination
