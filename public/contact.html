How to Prepare

A Few Things Worth Thinking About

You don't need to have everything figured out — but the more context you can share, the better we can guide you. Here's what helps us understand your project from the start:

  • Property Type

    Is it an apartment, a house, or a commercial space? Owned or rental? This shapes the scope and materials we recommend.

  • Renovation Goal

    Are you preparing to sell, rent, or simply upgrade your living space? Your goal directly influences our strategy and prioritization.

  • Timeline

    Do you have a target move-in date, a listing deadline, or a lease start? Knowing your timeline helps us plan resources and scheduling effectively.

  • General Scope

    Full renovation or targeted improvements? Painting only, or structural work too? Even a rough idea of the areas involved is a great starting point.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your renovation project with our interior design company.

What types of projects do you handle?

We manage a full spectrum of renovation work — from single-room refreshes and painting to complete apartment and office overhauls. Whether you are preparing a property for sale, rental, or personal use, we tailor the scope to your goals and budget.

How quickly can I receive a quote?

After an initial consultation — either on-site or by phone — we typically provide a detailed written quote within 24 to 48 hours. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees, so every line item is clearly outlined before work begins.

How should I prepare before work starts?

We guide you through every preparation step during the planning phase. Generally, this means clearing the work area of personal items and furniture, confirming material selections in advance, and ensuring access to water and electricity. Our team handles all protective covering and site setup.

Do you help with design and finishing choices?

Absolutely. Our renovation and interior design company provides full design guidance — from color palette selection and material finishes to fixtures and flooring. We help you make decisions that enhance both the aesthetic appeal and the market value of your property.

How long does a typical renovation take?

Project timelines depend on scope and complexity. A focused painting job may take two to three days, while a full apartment renovation typically spans two to six weeks. We provide a clear schedule at the outset and keep you updated throughout every stage of the process.

Is the work guaranteed after completion?

Yes. We stand behind the quality of every project with a workmanship warranty. If any issue arises from our work after handover, we return promptly to address it at no additional cost. Client satisfaction is the foundation of our reputation.
